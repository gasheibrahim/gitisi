Our History

The idea to form the Organization for Women for the Familial Progress came from the views of MIFEM Adventist women living in Ruhango District, Bweramana and Ruhango Sector at various meetings held in Gitisi Evangelical Province, Central Rwanda Field ( Center Rwanda Field) in January 2002 studied issues affecting women and girls based on their inability to access secondary education and vocational education to be able to create lucrative jobs for their development. The idea came as a result of the establishment of a vocational school that was immediately born in 2002: it was the idea of ​​borrowing Adventist church buildings located in Kabugusu Village, Gitisi Cell, Bweramana Sector, Ruhango District in the Southern Province. It was then that AFEPROF began to establish its headquarters in Gitisi and started a vocational school at the Center de Formation des Metier de Gitisi (CFMG) in 2002.

The members who introduced it were only sixty-four even though it was the idea of ​​women not ending with men because they are now mixed and complementary; started a five-thousand-member association (5,000 Rwf); they come to increase their enrollment in the education project to the tune of three hundred thousand (300,000) now that even a new member pays if he wants to join and whoever wants to retire is the one who gets it; For further development, he has partnered with member development partners including Jumelage renani palatine (German) where he has helped us build strong buildings and help us on a daily basis as they are used in our Education project; The Adventist Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints because it has also provided us with some of the Buildings that have helped us to teach children very vocational trainings; MINEDUC / WDA; RDRC, CHF, AVIS, MIGEPROF, PRO-FEMMES ALL AND the local authorities we work with on a daily basis.

AFEPROF began teaching and helping its members build and develop in 2002, operating legally as it has RGB legal status, working with REB and WDA to provide knowledge and certification.
